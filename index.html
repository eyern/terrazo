Our Services

Polished Terrazzo Flooring

Elevate the shine and visual appeal of your terrazzo floors with our professional polishing services. Our experts bring out the natural brilliance of terrazzo, leaving you with a gleaming surface.

Residential Terrazzo Flooring

Create a luxurious and welcoming atmosphere in your home with our residential terrazzo flooring. From classic designs to contemporary styles, we bring your vision to life, making your living spaces truly extraordinary

Maintenance and Care

At Gideon Terrazzo, we believe that a well-maintained terrazzo floor can last a lifetime. Our experts provide guidance on the proper cleaning routine, which involves using a neutral pH cleaner, and we recommend occasional resealing to keep your terrazzo floor looking pristine.

Indoor Terrazzo Flooring

Indoor terrazzo flooring adds sophistication and durability to your interior spaces. We offer a wide range of colors, patterns, and finishes to complement your design preferences.
